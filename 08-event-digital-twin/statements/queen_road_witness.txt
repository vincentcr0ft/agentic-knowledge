I was waiting at the bus stop on Queen's Road, just south of the King Street junction, at around quarter past two on Tuesday afternoon. I saw a red hatchback come speeding through the junction from King Street and hit a man on a bicycle. The car didn't stop immediately — the driver, a tall man, stepped out briefly, looked around, then got back in and drove north up Queen's Road. The cyclist was lying on the road and seemed to be in pain but was conscious. A woman in a green coat who had been walking on the other side of the road ran over and called 999. I went to help too and we stayed until the ambulance arrived maybe eight or ten minutes later. The car's registration plate started with "KV" but I couldn't read the rest.
